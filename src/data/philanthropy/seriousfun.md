# SeriousFun Children’s Network

SeriousFun is a global network of medical specialty camps for children with serious illnesses and medical conditions. Across **30+ independent camps** around the world, kids from **50+ countries** get to experience camp **free of charge**—surrounded by community, joy, and expert medical support.

### Who they serve

Kids come to SeriousFun camps with a wide range of diagnoses and needs, including:

- Cancer and blood disorders
- Neurological conditions
- Immunological conditions
- And other serious and complex medical conditions

### Why we support SeriousFun

As part of our chapter philanthropy, **Phi Kappa Tau and Delta Zeta** raise money to help make camp possible for more kids and families.

Learn more about SeriousFun’s mission and impact on their website: `https://seriousfun.org/`.
